About

Hello and thank you for visiting my portfolio site. My name is Derek Thornton and I have been fascinated with science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) my whole life. My formal education was spent on the natural sciences where I first became obsessed with challenging phylosolphies and developing my own theories that I extended beyond physics, chemistry, and biology and began to apply to entrepreneurship. All of my ideas seemed to converge on app-based solutions for implementaion. I eventually realized that it was time to dive into the estranged field that I had always longed for, computer science.

It was an opportunity to also learn new languages. I loved and always craved more of the language of the universe, mathematics, then I grew insatiable for spoken languages and gained fluency in several, and then it was finally my time to learn coding languages. After being employed to work in a full-stack environment my passion only grew to develop my skills and create more diverse and complex applications.

"My satisfaction comes from the increasing sentiment of being able to build anything that I can conceive."
